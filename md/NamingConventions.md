## NamingConventions

- index of name

1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | Pitch | Octave
---|---|---|---|---|---|---
S/X/N | L/R/N | Color | U/D/N | Type | 1-7 | 0-8

- **S**, X, N for **\#**

- Type (A-F)
    1. A Normal
    2. B Tab/Ctrl
    3. C Enter / Caps / Backspace / Numpad_0
    4. D Shift
    5. E Spcae
    6. F Numpad_+ / Numpad_Enter

- Color 

code | Color
---|---
W | White
G | Green
L | Light Blue
B | Blue
R | bRown
    
- Side

Code | Side
---|---
L | Left
R | Right
N | Null

- Press & Release

Code | Direction
---|---
U | Up
D | Down
N | Null

- Pitch

Pitch | Number
---|---
C | 1
D | 2
E | 3
F | 4
G | 5
A | 6
B | 7

- Octave

Octave | Number
---|---
-4 | 0
-3 | 1
-2 | 2
-1 | 3
0 | 4
1 | 5
2 | 6
3 | 7
4 | 8
